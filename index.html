About

A Natural Eye
for Beautiful Spaces

Hi, I'm Sophia — and I believe your home should be the best place you've ever been.

I started Timeless Living because I've always had a deep love for the way a room can make you feel. The way the right light, the right texture, the right piece of furniture can shift everything. It's not about following trends — it's about understanding you.

Every project I take on begins with listening. I want to know how you live, what brings you joy, and what a transformed space would mean for your day-to-day life. From there, the design unfolds naturally — warm, considered, and completely yours.

Services

What We Do Together

Every project is tailored to your space, your story, and your vision — from a single room refresh to a full home transformation.

Full Room Makeovers

A complete reimagining of your space — layout, palette, furnishings, and styling — crafted to feel timeless and entirely you.

Space Planning

Flow matters. I'll help you understand how to arrange your space so it feels effortless to move through and beautifully functional.

Color Consultation

Color is everything. From wall paint to textiles to accents, I'll guide you to a palette that feels warm, cohesive, and alive in your light.

Furniture Sourcing

Skip the hours of searching. I'll curate and source the exact pieces that fit your aesthetic, your space, and your budget.

Styling & Staging

The details make the difference. Books, ceramics, botanicals, art — the finishing touches that make a room feel complete and curated.

How It Works

Simple. Personal. Transformative.

From your first message to the final flourish, every step is thoughtful, collaborative, and — dare we say — enjoyable.

01

Consultation

We start with a conversation — not a questionnaire. Tell me about your space, your life, and what you dream your home could feel like. I'll listen, and we'll figure out the rest together.

02

Design Plan

I create a tailored design plan — palette, layout, mood boards, and a curated selection of furniture and accents — that captures your vision and gives your space direction and soul.

03

Transform

This is where the magic happens. We bring the plan to life — and when we're done, you'll walk into your home and feel it differently. That's the Timeless Living promise.
